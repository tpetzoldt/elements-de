Description: Size of Clementine Orange samples

Purpose: Statistics Course

Source: The data are from a students course experiments.

clementines2017.csv: November 2017
clementines.csv:     November 2020

Samples of Clementines were bought in different shops, 
then weight (in g), width and height (in mm) were measured.

Note: as complete bags of fruits were bought in the shops, 
  representativity has limited value. This is part of the
  discussion in the course.
